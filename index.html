Frequently Asked Questions

Some common questions clients ask before working with us.

Do you charge consultation fees?

No, our initial consultation is completely free. We believe in helping you understand your options before making any commitment.

Are you tied to any specific bank or insurance company?

No. We are independent advisors who compare options across multiple banks and insurers to find the best fit for your needs.

How do you get paid?

For most services, we are compensated directly by banks or insurers—so you receive our advisory at no additional cost.

How long does a mortgage or SME loan approval take?

Typically 3–14 working days depending on documents, bank policies and case complexity.

What documents do I need for a mortgage loan?

Common documents include payslips, EPF, bank statements, tax forms and property details. We’ll provide a full checklist based on your profile.

Is there any obligation if I enquire?

No, there is absolutely no obligation. You decide only after reviewing your options.
